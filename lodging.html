BED & BREAKFAST

Enjoy a cozy, local stay at one of Tanini’s family-run bed and breakfasts. These smaller properties offer comfortable rooms, homemade breakfasts, and a chance to experience Tanitian hospitality firsthand.

HOTELS

Tanini’s hotels range from budget-friendly options to mid-size family-owned properties. Guests can expect convenient locations, modern amenities, and easy access to beaches, dining, and local attractions.

RESORT

Tanini is home to one large, four-star resort offering upscale accommodations, on-site dining, pools, and guided excursions. This option is ideal for travelers seeking a full-service island getaway.

*All lodging options in Tanini are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
